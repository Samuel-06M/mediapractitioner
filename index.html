Projects

Here are a few projects I worked on during my time at university, showcasing my skills in media production and communication.

Documentary Film: "Voices of Change"

Produced a 15-minute documentary film titled "Voices of Change," which highlights the impact of social media on youth activism in Kenya. I was responsible for the entire production process, including scriptwriting, filming, editing, and post-production. The documentary was featured at the university's annual media festival and received positive feedback for its compelling storytelling and visual quality.

Social Media Campaign: "Green Future"

Developed and executed a social media campaign called "Green Future" to raise awareness about environmental conservation. The campaign included the creation of engaging content, such as infographics, videos, and articles, which were shared across various platforms like Facebook, Twitter, and Instagram. The campaign successfully reached over 10,000 people and sparked conversations about sustainable practices in our community.

University Radio Show: "The Media Hour"

Hosted and produced a weekly radio show called "The Media Hour" on the university's radio station. The show focused on current trends in media, interviews with industry professionals, and discussions on the role of media in society. I was responsible for content creation, guest coordination, and live broadcasting. The show was popular among students and became a platform for meaningful dialogue about media issues.

Public Relations Campaign: "Campus Voices"

Led a public relations campaign called "Campus Voices" to improve student engagement and communication on campus. The campaign involved organizing events, creating promotional materials, and managing media relations. The campaign resulted in increased student participation in campus activities and enhanced the university's public image.
